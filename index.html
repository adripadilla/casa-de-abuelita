About Rachel

For over four decades, Rachel has poured her heart into sewing, design, and cooking for her community. From custom-made dresses and quinceañera gowns to handmade tamales and catering for special events, her creativity knows no limits.

What began as a small home sewing studio has grown into Casa de Abuelita — a place where every stitch and every meal is made with love, tradition, and care.
